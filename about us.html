Kaya Twi Secondary School Description

Kaya tiwi is an extra county school in coast province located in south coast in kwale county.It is along lungalunga highway near Ukunda town in Tiwi.
Kaya tiwi secondary school is a kenyan secondary school that is designated as a public institution.
it is located in Matuga constituency , Mkonyo sub-location , Tiwi location in Matunga Division of kwale District. This is within the coast province of Kenya.
The student population comprises of mixed students and the population is 1500.
studies are carried out through a kenyan based curricum. The school aims to prepare students to make a positive contribution to the development of society and to acquire the values of national patriotism ,self-respect, self-reliance, cooperation ,adaptability, a sense of purpose ,and self-discipline.

School Curriculum

The secondary school curriculum in Kenya covers six major areas:

  1. Mathematics
  2. Communication:English, Kiswahili and foreign languages.
  3. Science:Physical Science and Biological Science.
  4. Humanities:History,Government, Social education,
    Geography,Religious education and ethics.
  5. Applied EducationAgriculture, Business education,
    Home science, commerce,industrial education and arts.
  6. Physical Education.

Form One Selection and Admissions

Form one selection is strictly done via academic merit and ministry of education guidelines.
Most of the time students who have high KCPE marks are given first priority.

Kaya Tiwi Secondary School KCSE results.

Individual candidates can check thier KCSE results by SMS or online using an SMS number or website link provided at the release of the exam.
Candidates and parents should visit the school for their offcial results slip.
Results for all candidates and the school;s natiol, county and local
ranking can also be found in the schools' office.
The ministry of education advises parentys teachers and pupils to check
the KCSE results slip for any anomaly such as incorrect candidate's name ,
